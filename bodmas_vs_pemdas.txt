1.1 - What is the precedence hierarchy in BODMAS? (2 marks)
B - Brackets
O - Order
D - Division
M - Multiplication
A - Addition
S - Subtraction

1.2 - List two rules for writing unambiguous expressions. (2 marks)
Parenthesize and use proper formatting, e.g. fraction bars

1.3 - What is the difference between BODMAS and PEMDAS? (1 mark)
BODMAS is commonly used in the UK and PEMDAS is commonly used in the USA.

2 + 3 - 6 / 3
